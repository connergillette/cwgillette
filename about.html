Bio

Lately I've been exploring interests in web design and web app development, and my work at efelle creative has helped hone my skills in that area.

I constantly like to be in a state of learning, whether it be something that will help me academically, in my career, or in general. I hope to pursue opportunities in the future that will help me learn new things and do meaningful work in the process.
